# andro_mobile

Andro is a constellation of ALU students connected by opportunity, collaboration, and purpose. Just like stars are scattered across the Andromeda Galaxy, ALU students will become more powerful through meaningful connections.

Andro is here to make opportunities, clubs, projects, mentorship, and communities easily discovered and accessed.

The Andromeda Galaxy and the Milky Way are destined to collide billions of years from now, creating something entirely new. In the same spirit, Andro brings together diverse minds, ideas, and ambitions today—creating collisions that spark innovation, leadership, and lifelong relationships.

Every student is a star. Together, we are Andro.
